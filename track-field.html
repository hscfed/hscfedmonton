Track & Field

Every year, HSCF hosts Track & Field at the Strathcona Athletic Park in Sherwood Park. This event is typically held the first Monday in June.

Children ages 5 through 18 are invited to participate.

Track & Field is for both HSCF members and Non-HSCF members.

  • HSCF Members: $5 per child; to a maximum of $20 per family.
  • Non-HSCF Members: $15 per child; to a maximum of $45 per family.

A volunteer from each family is required to make this event possible! Thank you for helping!

To register, please complete a single Parent Registration Form below and then complete a Child Registration Form for EACH child participating.

Registration typically opens at the beginning of May and closes a week before the event.
